About Our Church

Discover our story, mission, and the values that guide us.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Kingdom Covenant Family Church began as a small Bible study group in a living room with a vision to create a community of believers who would impact the world with God's love.

What started as a handful of people gathering to study God's Word has grown into a vibrant, multi-generational church family. Over the years, we've seen countless lives transformed by the power of the Gospel.

Today, we continue to be a place where people from all walks of life can encounter Jesus, grow in their faith, and use their gifts to serve others.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ by loving God, loving others, and serving the world with compassion and truth.

Our Vision

To be a thriving, multi-ethnic community where lives are transformed through the power of the Gospel, equipping believers to impact their world for Christ.

Our Core Values

These values guide everything we do as a church family.

Biblical Authority

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and the final authority for faith and life.

Authentic Community

We pursue meaningful relationships where we can grow, serve, and do life together.

Spiritual Growth

We are committed to helping every believer grow in their relationship with Christ.

Generous Living

We give generously of our time, talents, and resources to advance God's kingdom.

Global Impact

We are committed to taking the Gospel to our local community and the ends of the earth.

Compassionate Service

We demonstrate God's love by meeting the practical and spiritual needs of others.
